Join Us

We are actively seeking motivated and creative scientists to join our team. Applicants with strong interests and/or training in materials science, chemistry, environmental science, and chemical engineering are welcome.

Who we are looking for

  • Solid academic training in materials, chemistry, environmental science, chemical engineering, or related fields
  • Strong ability to conduct independent research and prepare scientific manuscripts; proficiency in English reading and writing
  • Prior research experience in electrothermal technologies, resource circularity, environmental remediation, or functional materials is a plus

How to apply

Please contact Dr. Yi Cheng via email: yi_cheng212@163.com.

Postdoctoral researchers

  • (1) CV
  • (2) A one-page summary of research interests and background (figures allowed)
  • (3) Two representative publications

Email subject: Postdoc Application + Your Name

Graduate students

  • (1) CV
  • (2) A brief description of research interests and background

Email subject: Graduate Student Inquiry + Your Name

Research assistants

  • (1) CV
  • (2) A brief summary of research experiences and skillsets

Email subject: Research Assistant Application + Your Name

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an online meeting.
