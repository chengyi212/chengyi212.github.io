Cheng Research Lab

Yi Cheng | Incoming Assistant Professor

Laboratory of Advanced Materials, College of Smart Materials and Future Energy, Fudan University
Yi Cheng is an incoming Assistant Professor of the Laboratory of Advanced Materials, College of Smart Materials and Future Energy, Fudan University. He received his B.S. degree from Fudan University and his Ph.D. degree from Peking University. He subsequently carried out postdoctoral research at Rice University. He has published over 50 peer-reviewed journal articles, including more than 20 papers as first or corresponding author in leading journals such as Nat. Sustain., Nat. Water, Nat. Chem. Eng., Nat. Commun., JACS, Matter, EES and ACS Nano. He is also an inventor on more than 30 granted or filed patents, with several technologies currently advancing toward industrial implementation.

Research Directions

Our group operates at the intersection of materials science, chemistry, and environmental engineering, with a particular focus on Flash Joule Heating (FJH) and other related extreme-condition synthesis technologies. We investigate transformation mechanisms under extreme, transient electrothermal conditions, aiming to develop efficient, sustainable, and scalable strategies for solid waste valorization, resource recovery, and functional material synthesis.
